Disclaimer

Before you follow a route, book a flight, or trust a story on Roadmere — a few things worth knowing.

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Short version: Roadmere earns commissions when you book flights, hotels, gear, or trips through some of the links on this site. It doesn't cost you anything extra, and it's how we keep the maps, stories, and route planning free. We only recommend things we'd actually put in our own glovebox.

01 Affiliate disclosure

Roadmere participates in affiliate marketing programs, including partnerships with flight booking services, hotel and lodging platforms, travel gear retailers, and trip-planning tools. This means that when you click certain links on this site and make a purchase or booking, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links may appear in:

  • Route and trip listings on the Routes page
  • The flight search and trip planner tools
  • Traveler stories and mile-marker features
  • Gear, packing, and vehicle recommendations mentioned in articles

We disclose this in line with FTC guidelines on endorsements and testimonials in advertising. If a link is sponsored, an affiliate link, or part of a paid partnership, we aim to make that clear at the point where you encounter it — not buried somewhere you'd have to go looking for it.

02 Editorial independence

Commissions never determine what we cover, how we rate a route, or which traveler stories we publish. A route, hotel, or piece of gear earns its place on Roadmere because it's genuinely useful, not because it pays the highest commission. Where we have a financial relationship with a company mentioned on the site, that doesn't change our assessment of it.

03 Travel and route information

Routes, mileage, driving times, road conditions, and points of interest on Roadmere are provided for general planning purposes only. Conditions change — roads close, businesses shut down, borders and entry requirements shift, and weather can make a route unsafe with little warning.

  • Always verify current road conditions, closures, and travel advisories with official sources before you leave.
  • Confirm opening hours, availability, and entry requirements for any stop directly with the business or venue.
  • Use your own judgment about your vehicle's capability, your driving experience, and local laws.

Roadmere is not liable for delays, expenses, injury, or loss arising from reliance on route information, third-party bookings, or traveler stories published on this site.

Stories published on Roadmere are told from individual travelers' experiences and reflect a single trip at a single point in time. They are not a guarantee that your trip will unfold the same way. Opinions expressed in stories are the author's own and don't necessarily reflect Roadmere's views.

05 Flight and booking partners

Flight search results, prices, and availability shown through Roadmere are supplied by third-party booking partners and can change between the time you search and the time you book. Roadmere does not set prices, control availability, or process payments for flights or accommodations — you'll complete those bookings directly with the airline, hotel, or partner platform, under their own terms and cancellation policies.

06 No professional advice

Nothing on Roadmere constitutes legal, financial, medical, or safety advice. Content about border crossings, vehicle requirements, insurance, or health precautions is general in nature. For anything trip-critical, consult the relevant official agency, your insurer, or a qualified professional.

Roadmere links to third-party websites, including affiliate partners, maps, and businesses along a route. We don't control those sites and aren't responsible for their content, accuracy, or availability. A link doesn't imply a full endorsement of everything on the destination site.
